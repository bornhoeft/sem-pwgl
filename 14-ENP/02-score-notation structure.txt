The structure of mensurally notated score:

(score
 (part
  (voice
   (measure
    beat
    beat)
   (measure
    beat
    beat)
   )
  )
 )
    
The structure of non-mensurally notated score:

(score
 (part
  (voice
   chord
   chord
   )
  )
